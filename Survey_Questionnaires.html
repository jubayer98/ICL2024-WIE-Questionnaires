Instruction and Policy

This survey is conducted solely for academic research purposes. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality and data protection. Please carefully review the following instructions and policies before participating in the survey.

  1. Purpose and Confidentiality: The information collected through this survey is intended exclusively for academic research. We assure you that your responses will remain confidential and will not be disclosed to any third parties. The data will be aggregated and analyzed to ensure that individual responses cannot be identified.

  2. Participant Criteria: Participation in this survey is restricted to individuals who identify as female. This criterion is essential to the scope of the research and ensures the relevance and accuracy of the data collected.

  3. Educational Background: Eligibility to participate in this survey requires current or previous enrollment in an engineering education program. This includes those who have completed their studies as well as those who are actively pursuing their education in this field. This criterion is in place to ensure that the survey results accurately reflect the perspectives of those with experience in engineering education.

  4. Advanced Educational Status: Participants should either be currently engaged in or have completed higher education. This includes postgraduate studies, doctoral programs, or any form of advanced academic pursuit beyond the undergraduate level. This requirement is designed to gather insights from individuals who have advanced their academic journey, contributing depth and experience to the research findings.
